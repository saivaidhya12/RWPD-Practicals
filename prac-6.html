Student Activities Table

Debate Club
The Debate Club hones critical thinking and communication skills. Through structured debates, students learn to articulate ideas, defend viewpoints, and think on their feet. It fosters confidence in public speaking, research, and persuasion, while promoting open-mindedness
Science Olympiad
Science Olympiad caters to STEM enthusiasts. Students compete, conduct experiments, and solve challenging problems. It deepens STEM knowledge, fosters teamwork, and nurtures problem-solving abilities, preparing future scientists and engineers.
Art Club
The Art Club nurtures visual creativity. Students explore painting, drawing, sculpture, and digital art in a collaborative setting. It encourages self-expression, artistic growth, and the appreciation of aesthetics and culture.
Environmental Club
The Environmental Club raises eco-awareness. Students lead tree planting, recycling drives, and conservation efforts. They educate through events, lectures, and workshops, advocating for a greener, sustainable future.
Coding and Robotics Club
The Coding and Robotics Club introduces coding and robotics. It enhances problem-solving, logical thinking, and creativity. Students build robots, code, and often compete, igniting a passion for tech and innovation.
Music Ensemble
The Music Ensemble offers a platform for students to explore their musical talents and appreciation. Participants can learn to play various instruments, sing in a choir, or join a band. This co-curricular activity promotes teamwork, discipline, and artistic expression while nurturing a lifelong love for music. Students often have opportunities to perform in school concerts and events, showcasing their musical achievements to the school community and beyond.
